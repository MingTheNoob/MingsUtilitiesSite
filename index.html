The future of MingsUtilities

MingsUtilities is going to be focusing on a more sustainable software suite, that can be used and adapted for BlitzCream when the time comes. This means all Discord and Minecraft service plans have been cancelled. Our main focus will be on developing gaming/privacy related software.
